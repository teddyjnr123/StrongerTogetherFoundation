Thank you for supporting Stronger Together Foundation. Your donation funds direct, local interventions for children: school supplies, health screenings, sanitation improvements and structured activities that promote learning and wellbeing. We value every contribution and ensure donors receive clear, timely reporting about how funds are used.

How donations are used: We allocate funds according to programme priorities and publish quarterly breakdowns. Small donations are pooled to fund school kits and programme essentials; larger gifts may support infrastructure improvements or sustainably funded programmes.

NOTE: The site currently operates in demo mode. To accept live payments we will add secure server-side integration with a payment gateway (Paystack, Flutterwave or Hubtel). We do not store card details or secret keys in client-side code.
